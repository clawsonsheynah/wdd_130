Crocheting is fun

The things I love to crochet are dish cloths and coasters. A fun fact is the difference between crocheting and knitting is that in crochet, each stitch is completed before moving onto the next. In knitting, a large number of stitches may be kept open at once. Another fun fact about crocheting is that it has been proven to help with insomnia, stress, anxiety, and depression.
